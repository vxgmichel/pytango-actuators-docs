VacuumValve Device Documentation¶

class actuators.VacuumValve¶

Tango Device for a vacuum valve.

Device states description:
  • DevState.OPEN: the valve is open
  • DevState.CLOSE: the valve is closed
  • DevState.MOVING: intermediary position
  • DevState.FAULT: conflict or communication error
