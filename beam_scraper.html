BeamScraper Device Documentation¶

class actuators.BeamScraper¶

Tango Device for a beam scraper.

Device states description:
  • DevState.INSERT: the scraper is inserted
  • DevState.EXTRACT: the scraper is extracted
  • DevState.MOVING: intermediary position
  • DevState.FAULT: communication error
