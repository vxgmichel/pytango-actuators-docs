CameraScreen Device Documentation¶

class actuators.CameraScreen¶

Tango Device camera screen.

Device states description:
  • DevState.INSERT: the screen is inserted
  • DevState.EXTRACT: the screen is extracted
  • DevState.MOVING: intermediary position
  • DevState.FAULT: communication error
