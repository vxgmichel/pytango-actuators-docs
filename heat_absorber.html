HeatAbsorber Device Documentation¶

class actuators.HeatAbsorber¶

Tango Device for a head absorber.

Device states description:
  • DevState.OPEN: the absorber is open
  • DevState.CLOSE: the absorber is closed
  • DevState.MOVING: intermediary position
  • DevState.FAULT: conflict or communication error
