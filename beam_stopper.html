BeamStopper Device Documentation¶

class actuators.BeamStopper¶

Tango Device for a beam stopper controlled by a PSS PLC.

Device states description:
  • DevState.OPEN: status open
  • DevState.CLOSE: status closed
  • DevState.MOVING: intermediary position
  • DevState.DISABLE: not ok to open
  • DevState.FAULT: error
